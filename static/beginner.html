About Beginner MCUs

Beginner MCUs, or microcontrollers, are a great starting point for learning embedded systems and programming. They provide a simple and affordable way to get started with physical computing projects.

Arduino Uno

The Arduino Uno is one of the most popular beginner-friendly MCUs. It features an Atmega328P microcontroller and a user-friendly development environment. The Arduino Uno is known for its ease of use, extensive community support, and a wide range of compatible shields and modules.
